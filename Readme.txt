Speedhack 2014 entry by SiegeLord.

# Summary

You are a planet photographing spaceships while hurling away in the
gravity wells of stars and black holes!

# Controls

Arrows - Use thrusters
Space -- Take a picture of a spaceship (if it is in range)
R ------ Restart mission
Esc ---- Quit

# Rule explanations

## Traveling Salesman

Getting the spaceships in different orders will net you differing 
amounts of points, but otherwise does not preclude you from advancing.

## Character Development

Can trade score for extra fuel and better camera.

## Easter Egg

Go through the black hole to reverse the Soviet Russia rule.

## Player Customization

Can choose what planet you appear as. No effect on gameplay.

## Soviet Russia

In Soviet Russia planet photographs the space probe!

## Act of Efficiency

Not invoked.
